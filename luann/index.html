All About Luann

The Early Days

Luann is a Medical Laboratory Scientist at Mayo. He works in the transfusion lab processing blood products for patients. Luann grew up in New Hampton, a small town in Northeast Iowa, where she attended New Hampton high school. She went on to graduate from Wartburg College in 2011. She then got her Medical Laboratory Scientist license from Mayo, where she now works.
